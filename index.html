Welcome to Burger Haven

About Us

At Burger Haven, we're passionate about crafting the perfect burger experience for our customers. Our journey began with a simple idea: to create burgers that are not just delicious but also made with the freshest ingredients and cooked to perfection.

Menu

  1. BBQ Bacon Burger: Beef patty topped with crispy bacon, BBQ sauce, cheddar cheese, lettuce, and onion rings.
  2. Spicy Jalapeño Burger: Angus beef patty with spicy jalapeños, pepper jack cheese, chipotle mayo, lettuce, and tomato.
  3. Hawaiian Teriyaki Burger: Grilled chicken breast with teriyaki sauce, grilled pineapple, Swiss cheese, lettuce, and mayo.
  4. Mediterranean Lamb Burger: Seasoned lamb patty, feta cheese, cucumber, tomato, red onion, and tzatziki sauce.
  5. Breakfast Burger: Beef patty, fried egg, crispy hash browns, American cheese, bacon, and ketchup.
  6. Southwest Chipotle Burger: Grilled chicken or beef patty with chipotle aioli, avocado slices, pepper jack cheese, lettuce, and tomato.
